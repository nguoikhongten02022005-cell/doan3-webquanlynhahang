# 08 Report / Slide Writer

## Cach viet
- Ngan gon.
- De thuyet trinh.
- Noi dung phai khop repo that.
- Moi slide 3-5 y chinh.

## Cau truc slide de xuat
1. Tieu de de tai
2. Muc tieu
3. Pham vi
4. Cong nghe
5. Kien truc / use case tong quan
6. CSDL / ERD
7. Chuc nang khach hang
8. Chuc nang admin/noi bo
9. Demo he thong
10. Ket qua dat duoc
11. Han che
12. Huong phat trien
13. Ket luan

## Noi dung nen co
- Muc tieu he thong
- Pham vi bai toan
- Stack FE / BE / DB
- Use case tong quan
- Bieu do lop / ERD
- Luong khach hang
- Luong noi bo
- Cac man hinh chinh
- Cac bang DB chinh
- Trang thai nghiep vu

## Format moi slide
- **Tieu de**
- 3-5 y chinh
- Ghi chu thuyet trinh ngan

## Luu y
- Khong phóng dai tinh nang chua co.
- Khong noi hoan thien end-to-end neu chi la demo mot phan.
- Neu can, tach 'da co' va 'huong phat trien' ro rang.
